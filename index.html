About

Hello! I'm Ashley Kang (she/her). I use design, code, and pedagogy to ask questions and build narratives that connect people, culture, and information.

As a teen on Tumblr, undergrad, public and after school educator, intern, apprentice, remote consultant, digital agency worker, and Square Code Camper, I've been convinced the coolest thing is to learn how to use technologies to discover and pursue what you care about. These days, I'm taking postgrad coursework in computer science and working as an after school educator and a university marketing team's first web developer fellow. Ask me about designing my own undergrad major in "Visual + Digital Culture" and being a member of a technology learning collective known as Color Coded.
